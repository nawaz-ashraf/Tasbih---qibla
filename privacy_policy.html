1. Overview

Tasbih & Qibla ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is developed and maintained by Nawaz Ashraf. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed primarily to function with minimal data collection. We only access data that is strictly necessary to provide you with our core features — Tasbih (prayer counter) and Qibla direction finder.

By using Tasbih & Qibla, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.
📦

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Location Data: We access your device's GPS / network location solely to determine the Qibla direction and your local prayer times. This data is processed on-device and is not stored on any external server.
  • Device Sensors: We use the device's compass (magnetometer) and accelerometer to calculate the Qibla bearing in real time.
  • Local Storage / Preferences: We save your Tasbih count, selected dhikr, theme preference, and language settings locally on your device using Shared Preferences. This data never leaves your device.
  • Ad-related Data: Google AdMob may collect certain device identifiers and usage information to serve personalised or non-personalised advertisements. See the Advertising section for details.
No Account Required No Server Storage No Tracking Across Apps
📍

3. Location Data

The App requests Access Fine Location and Access Coarse Location permissions to determine:

  • Your geographic coordinates to calculate the direction of the Kaaba (Qibla).
  • Your city/country for display purposes.

Location data is used only in real time and is never stored on our servers, shared with third parties, or used for advertising targeting by us. Third-party advertising SDKs (like Google AdMob) may independently access approximate location data based on your device's IP address to serve geographically relevant ads.

You may deny the location permission at any time from your device settings. The Tasbih counter feature will continue to work without location access; however, the Qibla feature will be unavailable.

📢

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App is free to use and is supported by in-app advertisements provided by Google AdMob (a service of Google LLC). AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (GAID on Android)
  • Device model and OS version
  • IP address (for approximate geolocation)
  • App interaction events
  • User consent signals (via UMP/GDPR framework)

We use AdMob's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect your consent before showing personalised ads in regions where GDPR or similar regulations apply. You can withdraw your consent at any time through your device's ad-settings or by resetting your Advertising ID.

We display the following ad formats: Banner Ads, Interstitial Ads, and App Open Ads. Ads are served in compliance with Google's Publisher Policies.

For more information on how Google uses data from apps that use AdMob, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party libraries and services:

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at Sr.nawaz446@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Data Security

Since the App does not transmit personal data to our servers, the risk of a data breach on our end is minimal. All user preferences and Tasbih state are stored locally on your device using Android's secure Shared Preferences mechanism.

For data handled by third-party services such as AdMob, we rely on their respective security infrastructure and compliance programs (including SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certifications maintained by Google).

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the following rights:

  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (uninstalling the App removes all local data instantly).

  • Withdraw consent: You can withdraw consent for personalised ads via the consent dialog in the App or through your device privacy settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at Sr.nawaz446@gmail.com.
